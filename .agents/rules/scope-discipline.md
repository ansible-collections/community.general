# Scope Discipline

- Stay strictly within the scope of the current task or branch. Do not add extra changes,
  improvements, or cleanups unless explicitly requested.
- Do not introduce deprecations as a side effect of other changes. Only add a deprecation
  when the branch/PR is specifically about that deprecation.
- When in doubt about whether a change is in scope, ask before making it.
